Guided steps to set up a Trezor Wallet

The process of setting up a Trezor wallet is very easy and convenient. It does not require much time on the part of users to complete it. All you need to do is, refer to the guiding steps given below:

  1. Begin the process by navigating to Trezor.io/start
  2. From the given page, select your Trezor Wallet model
  3. Now, you will be taken to the "Download" page
  4. From here, you can easily download the Trezor Bridge or the Trezor Suite
  5. Choose the download option based on the operating system of your device
  6. After done with the installation, you need to connect your Trezor device to your PC
  7. You can use a USB cable to connect the two devices and then follow the on-screen instructions
  8. After generating the seed phrase, create a secure backup of the same
